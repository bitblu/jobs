What we need

A CTO. Someone to take ownership of our entire technical operation, architecture, and security. You have to be top notch. You could get a job at Google or Facebook it you wanted too ... but you just love startups too damn much and are looking for the challenge of building a new company from scratch.

You can't be an asshole. We are going to have fun building our company, so please, if you can't play well with others, do not approach us.

We already have tons of knowledge about Bitcoin in the company, so you are not required to come with "The Bitcoin Craze" already in you ... you will learn the ropes very quickly.

You will build our entire technological stack from day one. You will build a secure and scalable system tha holds customers' funds, while also building our web front and and mobile app (you will be able to hire people to help you accomplish all this).

You will become passionate about Bitcoin ... we all are, and we believe you can't effectively work on something you're not passionate about (we'll be happy to share with you why we're passionate about Bitcoin).

We are an early stage startup

We have are gearign towards a pre-seed round, but have not raised any funds at this point. You will work for equity, and will receive the same equity as the other co-founders. You should be willing to commit 100% of your time, without receiving any salary, for at least 3-6 months.

We are (extremely) open and transparent

Ask us anything about our business, and we'll happily share with you. You should be ready to challenge common myths about building startups such as "secrecy is essential to build a successful company".

We are based in Israel, but recruiting globally

We are open to candidates from all locations. Currently our small team of co-founders is located in Israel, but are studying the distributed company structure used by companies such as Github and Treehouse,. If you're good enough, you won't have to relocate to Israel to work with us, and will be able to recruit your own team, either in your own area or globally.
